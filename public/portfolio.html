Electrician

Local Electrician — Orange County, CA

Residential & Commercial Electrical Services

The Challenge

A well-established local electrician with 15+ years of experience was struggling to compete with newer companies that had aggressive online marketing. Their Google Business Profile had only 8 reviews despite hundreds of satisfied customers, their website was outdated and not mobile-friendly, and they were spending $2,000/month on generic print ads with no way to track ROI. Lead volume had dropped 30% year-over-year, and they were losing bids to competitors who showed up first in local search results.

Our Solution

We deployed a comprehensive AI-powered marketing strategy starting with a complete website redesign optimized for local SEO. We built automated review request sequences that triggered after each completed job, set up Google Local Services Ads with AI-optimized bidding, and created a lead capture system with instant response automation. Our AI analyzed competitor keywords and ad spend to identify underserved niches in the local market, allowing us to target high-value commercial electrical work that competitors were ignoring.

The Challenge

When a major competitor launched an aggressive Google Ads campaign in their service area, their call volume dropped by nearly half in just two months. We launched targeted Google Ads focusing on high-intent emergency plumbing keywords in their specific service area, with AI managing bid optimization daily to maximize their limited budget.

Roofer

Local Roofer — Inland Empire, CA

Residential Roofing & Storm Damage Repair

The Challenge

A licensed roofing contractor in the Inland Empire was spending $3,500/month across three different marketing platforms with no unified tracking. They had no idea which campaigns were actually generating jobs. Their website was a template site with stock photos that looked identical to five other roofers in the area. Despite quality workmanship and proper licensing, they were being outranked by unlicensed competitors running aggressive pay-per-click campaigns. Their average job value was high ($8K-$15K), but lead quality from existing channels was poor — mostly tire-kickers requesting free estimates with no intent to hire.

Our Solution

We consolidated their marketing spend into a single AI-optimized system. First, we rebuilt their website with real project photos, video testimonials from past clients, and a pre-qualification form that filtered out low-intent leads before they consumed sales time. We set up Google Local Services Ads with their actual CSLB license number prominently displayed (a trust signal that unlicensed competitors can't match), and built an automated follow-up sequence that nurtured leads with case studies and before/after project galleries. Our AI monitored weather patterns and insurance claim data to adjust ad spend during peak demand periods like storm seasons.
